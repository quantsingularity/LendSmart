## Getting Started

LendSmart is a decentralized peer-to-peer lending platform that connects lenders directly with borrowers through blockchain technology. As a lender, you can fund loans with competitive returns, manage your investment portfolio, and diversify your lending across multiple borrowers.

### System Requirements

- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Ethereum wallet (MetaMask recommended)
- Stable internet connection
- Mobile or desktop device

## Creating an Account

1. **Visit the LendSmart Platform**:
    - Navigate to [www.lendsmart.io](https://www.lendsmart.io) in your web browser.
    - Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner.

2. **Registration Process**:
    - Enter your email address and create a strong password.
    - Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox.
    - Complete the basic profile information (name, country of residence).

3. **Connect Your Wallet**:
    - Click on "Connect Wallet" in your dashboard.
    - Select your preferred wallet provider (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.).
    - Follow the prompts to connect your wallet to the platform.
    - Verify your wallet address by signing a message.

## Completing Your Profile

A complete profile helps establish trust with borrowers and enables access to advanced lending features.

1. **Personal Information**:
    - Navigate to "Profile" in the sidebar menu.
    - Complete all required fields in the "Personal Information" section.
    - Upload a profile picture (optional but recommended).

2. **Identity Verification (KYC)**:
    - Go to the "Verification" tab in your profile.
    - Select your ID type (passport, driver's license, national ID).
    - Upload clear images of your identification document.
    - Take a selfie for facial verification.
    - Wait for verification approval (typically 24-48 hours).

3. **Lender Preferences**:
    - Set your risk tolerance level.
    - Define your preferred loan types and industries.
    - Set minimum and maximum investment amounts.
    - Configure automatic lending criteria (optional).

4. **Tax Information**:
    - Provide necessary tax information for your jurisdiction.
    - Upload any required tax documents.
    - Set your tax reporting preferences.

## Funding Loans

1. **Browsing the Loan Marketplace**:
    - Access the "Marketplace" from the sidebar menu.
    - View available loan listings with key information:
        - Loan amount and currency
        - Interest rate and term
        - Borrower reputation score
        - Loan purpose
        - Risk assessment rating
        - Funding progress

2. **Filtering and Sorting Loans**:
    - Use filters to narrow down loan options:
        - Amount range
        - Interest rate range
        - Loan term
        - Borrower reputation score
        - Risk level
        - Loan purpose
        - Currency
    - Sort loans by various criteria:
        - Interest rate (high to low)
        - Funding progress
        - Loan term (short to long)
        - Newest listings

3. **Analyzing Loan Opportunities**:
    - Click on a loan to view detailed information:
        - Borrower profile and history
        - Detailed loan purpose description
        - Risk assessment breakdown
        - Repayment schedule
        - Supporting documents
        - AI-generated risk insights

4. **Funding a Loan**:
    - From the loan details page, click "Fund This Loan".
    - Enter the amount you wish to contribute.
    - Review the investment summary:
        - Your contribution amount
        - Expected returns
        - Platform fees
        - Estimated repayment schedule
    - Confirm and sign the transaction with your wallet.
    - Wait for blockchain confirmation.

5. **Partial Funding**:
    - You can partially fund loans based on your risk management strategy.
    - Minimum funding amount is typically 5% of the total loan.
    - Maximum funding amount is typically 80% of the total loan (to encourage diversification).

6. **Auto-Lending**:
    - Configure auto-lending rules in your profile settings:
        - Set criteria for automatic investments
        - Define maximum exposure per borrower
        - Set daily/weekly investment limits
        - Define risk parameters
    - Enable/disable auto-lending as needed
    - Monitor auto-lending activity in your dashboard

## Receiving Repayments

1. **Repayment Schedule**:
    - View expected repayments in your portfolio dashboard.
    - Each repayment includes principal and interest components.
    - Scheduled repayment dates are clearly marked on your calendar.

2. **Repayment Process**:
    - Repayments are automatically processed through smart contracts.
    - Funds are directly deposited to your connected wallet.
    - You'll receive notifications for all repayments.

3. **Repayment Status Tracking**:
    - Monitor repayment status for each loan:
        - **Upcoming**: Future repayments not yet due
        - **Pending**: Repayments in processing
        - **Completed**: Successfully received repayments
        - **Late**: Repayments past due date but within grace period
        - **Defaulted**: Repayments past grace period

4. **Reinvestment Options**:
    - Automatically reinvest repayments into new loans.
    - Configure reinvestment criteria in your profile settings.
    - Manually allocate repaid funds to specific loans.

## Risk Management

1. **Understanding Risk Ratings**:
    - LendSmart uses an AI-powered risk assessment system:
        - **A+**: Lowest risk, highest creditworthiness
        - **A**: Very low risk
        - **B+**: Low risk
        - **B**: Moderate risk
        - **C+**: Moderate-high risk
        - **C**: High risk
        - **D**: Highest risk

2. **Diversification Strategies**:
    - Spread investments across multiple loans.
    - Diversify across risk categories.
    - Invest in different loan terms.
    - Distribute investments across industries.
    - Consider geographic diversification.

3. **Risk Mitigation Tools**:
    - **Loan Insurance**: Optional insurance for higher-risk loans.
    - **Provision Funds**: Platform-maintained fund to cover defaults.
    - **Secondary Market**: Sell loan parts before maturity.
    - **Auto-Diversification**: Automated diversification tools.

4. **Default Management**:
    - Understand the default recovery process:
        - Grace period handling
        - Collection procedures
        - Recovery rate expectations
        - Legal recourse options

5. **Risk Analytics**:
    - Access detailed risk analytics:
        - Expected default rates
        - Value at risk calculations
        - Stress test scenarios
        - Historical performance by risk category

## Security Best Practices

1. **Account Security**:
    - Use a strong, unique password.
    - Enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
    - Never share your private keys or recovery phrases.
    - Log out when using shared devices.

2. **Wallet Security**:
    - Use a hardware wallet for large transactions.
    - Regularly check connected applications.
    - Keep your wallet software updated.
    - Be cautious of phishing attempts.

3. **Transaction Verification**:
    - Always verify transaction details before signing.
    - Check recipient addresses carefully.
    - Confirm loan terms match the listing.
    - Report any suspicious activity immediately.

4. **Investment Security**:
    - Start with smaller investments to test the platform.
    - Gradually increase investment amounts as you gain confidence.
    - Never invest more than you can afford to lose.
    - Understand the risks associated with peer-to-peer lending.

## Troubleshooting

1. **Common Issues**:
    - **Wallet Connection Problems**: Refresh the page, ensure your wallet is unlocked, or try reconnecting.
    - **Transaction Failures**: Check your wallet balance, gas fees, and network congestion.
    - **Verification Delays**: Contact support if verification takes longer than 48 hours.
    - **Payment Processing Issues**: Verify transaction status on the blockchain explorer.
