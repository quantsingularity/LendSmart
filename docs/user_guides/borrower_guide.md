## Getting Started

LendSmart is a decentralized peer-to-peer lending platform that connects borrowers directly with lenders through blockchain technology. As a borrower, you can apply for loans with competitive interest rates, manage your repayments, and build your credit reputation.

### System Requirements

- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Ethereum wallet (MetaMask recommended)
- Stable internet connection
- Mobile or desktop device

## Creating an Account

1. **Visit the LendSmart Platform**:
   - Navigate to [www.lendsmart.io](https://www.lendsmart.io) in your web browser.
   - Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner.

2. **Registration Process**:
   - Enter your email address and create a strong password.
   - Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox.
   - Complete the basic profile information (name, country of residence).

3. **Connect Your Wallet**:
   - Click on "Connect Wallet" in your dashboard.
   - Select your preferred wallet provider (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.).
   - Follow the prompts to connect your wallet to the platform.
   - Verify your wallet address by signing a message.

## Completing Your Profile

A complete profile increases your chances of loan approval and may result in better interest rates.

1. **Personal Information**:
   - Navigate to "Profile" in the sidebar menu.
   - Complete all required fields in the "Personal Information" section.
   - Upload a profile picture (optional but recommended).

2. **Identity Verification (KYC)**:
   - Go to the "Verification" tab in your profile.
   - Select your ID type (passport, driver's license, national ID).
   - Upload clear images of your identification document.
   - Take a selfie for facial verification.
   - Wait for verification approval (typically 24-48 hours).

3. **Financial Information**:
   - Complete the "Financial Information" section.
   - Provide details about your income, employment, and expenses.
   - Link your bank account for repayments (optional).

4. **Additional Documentation**:
   - Upload proof of income (pay stubs, tax returns).
   - Provide proof of address (utility bill, bank statement).
   - Add any additional documents that may strengthen your application.

## Applying for a Loan

1. **Start a New Application**:
   - From your dashboard, click on "Apply for Loan".
   - Alternatively, navigate to the "Loans" section and click "New Application".

2. **Loan Details**:
   - Specify the loan amount you wish to borrow.
   - Select the loan term (duration).
   - Choose the currency for your loan.
   - Specify the purpose of the loan.

3. **Risk Assessment**:
   - The platform will automatically assess your risk profile.
   - This assessment determines your interest rate range.
   - Review the risk assessment results and suggested interest rates.

4. **Loan Proposal**:
   - Based on your risk assessment, create your loan proposal.
   - Set your preferred interest rate within the suggested range.
   - Add a description explaining your loan purpose and repayment plan.
   - Upload any supporting documents specific to this loan.

5. **Review and Submit**:
   - Review all loan details and terms.
   - Read and accept the loan agreement terms.
   - Sign the loan agreement using your connected wallet.
   - Submit your application to the marketplace.

6. **Funding Period**:
   - Your loan will be listed on the marketplace for lenders to fund.
   - You can track funding progress in your dashboard.
   - Loans can be partially funded by multiple lenders.
   - The funding period lasts up to 14 days.

7. **Loan Disbursement**:
   - Once your loan is fully funded, funds will be disbursed to your wallet.
   - You'll receive a notification when funds are available.
   - The smart contract will automatically handle the disbursement.

## Managing Your Loans

1. **Loan Dashboard**:
   - Access your loan dashboard from the sidebar menu.
   - View all your active, pending, and completed loans.
   - Monitor funding progress for pending applications.

2. **Loan Details**:
   - Click on any loan to view detailed information.
   - See lender information, repayment schedule, and loan terms.
   - Track your repayment history and upcoming payments.

3. **Loan Status Tracking**:
   - Monitor the status of your loans:
     - **Pending**: Loan application submitted but not fully funded.
     - **Active**: Loan funded and disbursed, repayments in progress.
     - **Completed**: All repayments made, loan fully repaid.
     - **Defaulted**: Missed repayments beyond grace period.

4. **Communication with Lenders**:
   - Use the messaging feature to communicate with lenders.
   - Respond to any questions or concerns promptly.
   - Maintain professional communication to build trust.

## Making Repayments

1. **Repayment Schedule**:
   - View your repayment schedule in the loan details page.
   - Each repayment includes principal and interest components.
   - Due dates are clearly marked on your calendar.

2. **Payment Methods**:
   - **Automatic Repayments**: Funds automatically transferred from your wallet on due dates.
   - **Manual Repayments**: Manually initiate payments before due dates.
   - **Early Repayments**: Pay off your loan early with potential interest savings.

3. **Making a Manual Repayment**:
   - Navigate to the loan details page.
   - Click on "Make Repayment".
   - Confirm the repayment amount.
   - Approve the transaction in your wallet.
   - Wait for blockchain confirmation.

4. **Early Repayment**:
   - Go to the loan details page.
   - Click on "Early Repayment".
   - Choose to repay a specific installment or the entire remaining balance.
   - Review any early repayment fees or savings.
   - Confirm and approve the transaction.

5. **Repayment Notifications**:
   - Receive reminders 3 days before payment due dates.
   - Get confirmation notifications after successful payments.
   - Receive alerts for any failed payment attempts.

6. **Handling Repayment Issues**:
   - If you anticipate difficulty making a repayment, contact support immediately.
   - Request a repayment extension if needed (subject to lender approval).
   - Understand the consequences of late or missed payments.

## Building Your Reputation

1. **Reputation Score**:
   - Your reputation score is visible on your profile.
   - Scores range from 0-100, with higher scores indicating better creditworthiness.
   - View detailed breakdown of your score components.

2. **Improving Your Score**:
   - Make all loan repayments on time.
   - Complete your profile with comprehensive information.
   - Maintain active platform participation.
   - Successfully repay multiple loans over time.

3. **Reputation Benefits**:
   - Higher reputation scores lead to:
     - Lower interest rates on future loans.
     - Higher borrowing limits.
     - Faster funding from lenders.
     - Access to premium borrower features.

4. **Reputation Recovery**:
   - If your score decreases due to late payments:
     - Make consistent on-time payments for several months.
     - Reduce your debt-to-income ratio.
     - Complete additional verification steps.
     - Participate in financial education programs.

## Using Multi-Currency Features

1. **Available Currencies**:
   - LendSmart supports multiple fiat and cryptocurrencies:
     - Fiat: USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, etc.
     - Crypto: ETH, USDC, DAI, etc.

2. **Currency Selection**:
   - Choose your preferred currency when applying for a loan.
   - View exchange rates and conversion fees.
   - Understand the implications of borrowing in different currencies.

3. **Currency Conversion**:
   - Convert between currencies in your wallet.
   - Understand the spread and conversion fees.
   - Monitor exchange rate fluctuations.

4. **Multi-Currency Repayments**:
   - Repay loans in the original currency or convert from another currency.
   - Set up automatic currency conversion for repayments.
   - Manage currency risk through hedging options.

## Notifications and Alerts

1. **Notification Types**:
   - **Loan Status Updates**: Changes in your loan application status.
   - **Funding Alerts**: When lenders fund your loan.
   - **Repayment Reminders**: Upcoming and due payments.
   - **Account Alerts**: Security and account updates.
   - **Platform News**: Important platform updates and features.

2. **Notification Settings**:
   - Customize notification preferences in your profile settings.
   - Choose delivery methods (email, SMS, in-app).
   - Set frequency and priority levels.

3. **Important Alerts**:
   - Some critical alerts cannot be disabled:
     - Payment due reminders
     - Successful/failed payment notifications
     - Security alerts
     - Loan disbursement notifications

## Security Best Practices

1. **Account Security**:
   - Use a strong, unique password.
   - Enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
   - Never share your private keys or recovery phrases.
   - Log out when using shared devices.

2. **Wallet Security**:
   - Use a hardware wallet for large transactions.
   - Regularly check connected applications.
   - Keep your wallet software updated.
   - Be cautious of phishing attempts.

3. **Transaction Verification**:
   - Always verify transaction details before signing.
   - Check recipient addresses carefully.
   - Confirm loan terms match your application.
   - Report any suspicious activity immediately.

4. **Privacy Protection**:
   - Understand what information is visible on the blockchain.
   - Review privacy settings in your profile.
   - Be mindful of information shared in loan descriptions.

## Troubleshooting

1. **Common Issues**:
   - **Wallet Connection Problems**: Refresh the page, ensure your wallet is unlocked, or try reconnecting.
   - **Transaction Failures**: Check your wallet balance, gas fees, and network congestion.
   - **Verification Delays**: Contact support if verification takes longer than 48 hours.
   - **Payment Processing Issues**: Verify your wallet has sufficient funds including gas fees.
